Why I Really Like Having Piercings

Here in Durban, I see lots of people showing their own style, and for me, piercings are a fun way to do that. It's not just about having metal on my body; it's about showing what I like and who I am.

Each piercing means something to me. One reminds me of a fun day with my friends at the beach. Another one just feels like it fits me. It's like decorating my own body in a way that feels right.

I also think the little shiny things look pretty. The way they sparkle a bit, it's like a small, cool detail that's just for me.
